Lasagne recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. This recipe offers a hearty and comforting meal perfect for family gatherings or a cozy dinner at home.

With its savory flavors and tender texture, this lasagna can be customized with your favorite ingredients, such as vegetables or different types of cheese, to suit your taste.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles in boiling water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan, add onion and garlic, and sauté until soft.
  4. Add ground beef, cook until browned, then stir in canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, followed by noodles, béchamel sauce, and cheese. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with remaining cheese and bake for 40 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
  7. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
