Authentic Tiramisu Recipe

Description

This authentic tiramisu recipe includes all the key components of the favorite Italian dessert: ladyfingers soaked in espresso, mascarpone, and cocoa.

It's a no-bake dessert that's rich, creamy, and perfect for special occasions, originating from Italy with a balance of coffee bitterness and sweet creaminess.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee or espresso
  • 1/4 cup brandy or rum (optional)
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 pound mascarpone cheese
  • 6 large egg whites
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • Unsalted cocoa powder for dusting

Steps

  1. Combine coffee and brandy in a shallow bowl; set aside to cool.
  2. Beat egg yolks and sugar in a large bowl until pale and thick, about 5 minutes.
  3. Gently fold mascarpone cheese into the egg yolk mixture until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gently fold into the mascarpone mixture.
  5. Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture (do not soak). Arrange half of the soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a 9x13-inch dish.
  6. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat layers with remaining ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture.
  7. Dust the top with cocoa powder. Refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours or overnight before serving.
